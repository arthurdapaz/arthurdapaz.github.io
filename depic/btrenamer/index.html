Cydia Package

  • BTRenamer for iOS 11 (may work at iOS 10, I don't know)

  • Just go to Preferences and long press a bluetooth device item and the options will popup
  • After renaming the tweak will write changes to the file com.apple.MobileBluetooth.devices.plist
